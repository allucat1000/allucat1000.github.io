The new, more modern, English

Verbs Words Irregular words
Primary verb: Take (singular) is took (plural).

Plural: replace the first vowel with two "O"s.

Past tense: add an "S" to the end of a word

Keep in mind, that these rules don't apply to "can" and "have". 		Primary words: Sheep (singular) is shoop (plural). Flower (singular), floweroot (plural).

Plural: if you have a word with two same vowels next to each other, replace them with "O"s. If they are "O"s, replace them with "E"s.

Otherwise, add "oot" to the end. If the original verb ends with a vowel, add a ' before the "oot". If the original verb ends with "oot" already, don't add "oot" again.		 Coming soon...!
Example: Pick is poock (current tense), poocks (past tense). Example: Plain is ploon, company is companyoot.
